Getting the Format right:


Recruiters scan thousands a CV a day. The average time they spend on one is roughly only 6 seconds!
Therefore, to sell yourself across, try to keep your CV:

  • concise
  • neat & simple
  • accurate
  • in chronological order

Check, check and check it Again!


As you'll be sending your CVs out, make sure it's truly ready before doing so!
This means, keep reviewing and proofreading it before sending it out!
You can also ask your family and friends around you to help out
They might be able to spot mistakes you missed!
