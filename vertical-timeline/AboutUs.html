What is Project Athena?



Project Athena was created to help female high school leavers & university graduates seek out and grab their dream job. Project Athena aims to guide and connect those who are in the same journey and make their dreams come true.



Who Are We?



Aileen and Becca are two university students in UCL who feel there needs to be more opportunities for women in the workplace. We hope that this website will be a helpful starting point for many girls out there, whether at the high school level or the college level. Let us know what you think at projectathena@email.com
